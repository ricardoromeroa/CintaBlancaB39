What does Climate Change do to us?

One of the reactions of Earth is a record in sea level rise - a strong 'acceleration' in Earth's sea levels rise. The outcome: worsening flooding in coastlines around Earth, more hurricanes and typhoons and changing earthquake patterns.

What could be driving current change?

  • Natural cycles: internal to the Earth's climate system
  • The sun: Irregular changes in energy from the Sun
  • The Orbit: Periodic cycles in the Earth's orbit around the Sun
  • Geology: Volcanoes, weathering, erosion
